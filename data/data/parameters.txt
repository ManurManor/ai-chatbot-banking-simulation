Simulation Input Parameters

Arrival process:
- Customer arrivals follow a Poisson process
- Average inter-arrival time: 3 minutes

Service mechanism:
- AI chatbot service times follow an exponential distribution
  Average service time: 2 minutes
- Human agent service times follow an exponential distribution
  Average service time: 5 minutes

Resources:
- Number of chatbot servers: 5
- Number of human agents: 2

Chatbot adoption rate:
- Base scenario: 60% of customers routed to chatbot
- Alternative scenarios adjust this value

Simulation horizon:
- 8-hour operating day (480 minutes)
